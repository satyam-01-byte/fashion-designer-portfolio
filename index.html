About me

An aspiring Luxury Wear Fashion designer

Fashion to me is an inspiring and a powerful message. It is an unparalleled medium which sets an individual apart allowing one to take the lead in their own story. I strongly believe in a design which empowers women to establish a sense of authority and a purposeful statement with a touch of elegance and femininity. My inspiration is a blend of modernity and history which comes from different forms of natural processes, art and cultures around me. I work around the idea of elevating classic feminine designs for the modern women with a purpose of establishing a beautiful story reciting the growth of one's perception regarding the role of a woman in today's time in our society. My designs are about finding a balance between the existing classic and the urban designs while being unconventional and unique. Through my design, I aim to establish a sense of motive, influence, inspiration and pleasure for women while being deeply rooted to the reality.
